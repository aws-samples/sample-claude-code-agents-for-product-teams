# approved incident comms

_Produced by: Approve Sev-1 external comms_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

## What this is

For Sev-1 incidents, the Sponsor approves the external customer communication before it is sent.

## Why it matters

Incident comms shape trust. The Sponsor's involvement ensures messaging reflects the business's stance, not just engineering's technical read.

## What it contains

- Draft comms, approved version, and delivery record
- Regulatory / legal notifications triggered

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Draft and approved comm versions are both recorded.
- [ ] A delivery record confirms the comm reached customers within the stated SLA.
- [ ] Regulatory or legal notifications triggered are listed with status.
- [ ] Sponsor approval is captured before delivery.

## Entry criteria

- Incident response underway
- Scope, cause, and mitigations known at time of comm

## Exit signal

Customers receive accurate, approved communication within the stated SLA.
