# business review decisions

_Produced by: Business review meeting_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Stakeholders:** Product Owner

**Accelerated MVP:** optional when decisions happen in-squad

## What this is

Decisions made in the recurring business review (Sponsor + PO + BA): on investment, pricing, expansion, or course-correction.

## Why it matters

Operate without a business-review cadence drifts into pure firefighting. The meeting creates regular, structured attention to the commercial engine.

## What it contains

- Decision list with owners and follow-ups
- Updated forecast or plan where applicable

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Each decision has a named owner and a follow-up action.
- [ ] Any resulting forecast or plan update is recorded alongside the decision.
- [ ] Decisions are dated and attributable to a specific business review.
- [ ] Decisions deferred or escalated are captured explicitly, not left implicit.

## Entry criteria

- Business performance report current
- Operational performance review current

## Exit signal

The product evolves through deliberate decisions, not default inertia.
