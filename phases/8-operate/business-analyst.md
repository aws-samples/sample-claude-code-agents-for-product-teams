# Business Analyst in Operate

Operate is where benefits realization stops being a plan and becomes evidence. You run the business-performance reporting cadence, track whether promised benefits are actually landing, and mine support and VoC data for trends the organization can act on.

## What you do

- Run the business-metric reporting cadence so the Sponsor and PO have a credible scorecard every period.
- Track value realization — are the benefits promised in the business case actually being earned?
- Analyze support and voice-of-customer data for trends, failure modes, and friction patterns.
- Contribute to the Sponsor's unit-economics review so margin conversations are grounded in data.

## Artifacts you own

- [business performance report](artifacts/business-performance-report.md) — the recurring business-metric readout.
- [ongoing benefits tracking](artifacts/ongoing-benefits-tracking.md) — the benefits-realization evidence log.
- [support/VoC trend analysis](artifacts/support-voc-trend-analysis.md) — the pattern-mining readout from support and VoC data.

## Artifacts you contribute to

- [margin & CAC/LTV report](artifacts/margin-cac-ltv-report.md) — Product Sponsor owns; you contribute unit-economics analysis.

## Outcomes you drive

You don't drive outcomes this phase — you input into others'.

## Who you work with

- **Product Sponsor** — bi-directional on business performance, margin, and benefits.
- **Product Owner** — bi-directional on business-performance signal and sequencing decisions.
- **Customer Support** — bi-directional on support data quality and VoC categorization.
- **Architect** — bi-directional on cost drivers and unit economics.

## Handoff into [Iterate](../9-iterate/README.md)

You exit Operate with a live evidence base on benefits, business performance, and VoC. Carry that into Iterate's benefits-realization review, adoption analysis, feedback insights, business-case reforecast, and shortfall root-cause work — evidence-based traceability is your contribution to keeping the next cycle honest.
