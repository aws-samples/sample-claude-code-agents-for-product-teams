# Product Owner in Operate

Operate is where your sequencing decisions meet the reality of real users. You co-chair the business review with the Product Sponsor, absorb operational and support signal, and keep the backlog honest so value-delivery decisions stay evidence-driven instead of opinion-driven.

## What you do

- Co-chair the business review meeting with the Product Sponsor to align portfolio and operational signal.
- Absorb support, incident, and KPI signal so backlog sequencing tracks real-customer reality.
- Make trade-off calls on operate-phase work (reactive fixes vs. planned feature investment).
- Feed Operate-phase learning into the Iterate roadmap refresh.

## Artifacts you own

You don't own any artifacts this phase — you contribute to others'.

## Artifacts you contribute to

You don't contribute to others' artifacts this phase.

## Outcomes you drive

- [business review decisions](outcomes/business-review-decisions.md) — your decisions, made with the Product Sponsor.

## Who you work with

- **Product Sponsor** — bi-directional on business review and portfolio posture.
- **Business Analyst** — bi-directional on business performance and benefits tracking.
- **Customer Support** — bi-directional on customer signal and health.
- **Site Reliability Engineer** — bi-directional on SLO performance and prioritized prod issues.
- **Architect** — bi-directional on architectural evolution and technical debt posture.

## Handoff into [Iterate](../9-iterate/README.md)

You exit Operate with live customer signal, operational evidence, and a backlog pressure-tested against real usage. Carry that into Iterate's roadmap refresh, KPI performance tracking, experimentation, and the next-cycle opportunity backlog — this is where you translate operating reality into the next wave of sequencing decisions.
