# Customer Support in Iterate

Iterate is where customer voice becomes product direction. You co-own the user feedback log and NPS with Sales & Marketing, translating a cycle of tickets, QBRs, and churn signal into the retention and opportunity inputs the next cycle needs. Your evidence is unfiltered reality.

## What you do

- Document user feedback with Sales & Marketing — the structured VoC record.
- Run customer success check-ins and NPS with Sales & Marketing.
- Feed churn-risk, activation-gap, and known-issue patterns into the next-cycle opportunity backlog.
- Translate support and CS reality into input for roadmap and deprecation decisions.

## Artifacts you own

- [user feedback log](artifacts/user-feedback-log.md) — the structured VoC record, with Sales & Marketing.
- [NPS & health scores](artifacts/nps-health-scores.md) — the NPS and customer-health readout, with Sales & Marketing.

## Artifacts you contribute to

You don't contribute to others' artifacts this phase.

## Outcomes you drive

You don't drive outcomes this phase — you input into others'.

## Who you work with

- **Sales & Marketing** — bi-directional on VoC, NPS, and customer health.
- **Product Owner** — bi-directional on retention-driving roadmap signal.
- **Business Analyst** — bi-directional on VoC trend analysis and feedback insights.
- **UI/UX Designer** — bi-directional on qualitative usability signal.

## Handoff into [the next Discover cycle](../1-discover/README.md)

You exit Iterate with the most honest customer evidence the company has. Carry that into the next Discover cycle's validated problem statement, user personas, and opportunity sizing — next-cycle discovery that's grounded in your evidence is sharper, faster, and less speculative than one that starts from scratch. That's how GRR and NRR compound cycle over cycle.
