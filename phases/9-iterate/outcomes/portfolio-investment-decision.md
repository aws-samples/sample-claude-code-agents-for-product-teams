# portfolio investment decision

_Produced by: Kill / pivot / double-down decision_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Accelerated MVP:** required — "iterate, pivot, double-down, or sunset" is the Iterate decision

## What this is

The Sponsor's decision on future investment in this product: kill, pivot, or double-down — informed by real performance vs. the business case.

## Why it matters

This is why the lifecycle loops exist. Without a decision point, every product keeps drifting on original momentum, regardless of whether it is earning its keep.

## What it contains

- Decision and rationale (evidence referenced)
- Updated roadmap and funding posture
- Communication plan for affected teams and customers

## Definition of Done

- [ ] A kill, pivot, or double-down decision is stated with rationale
- [ ] The decision cites referenced evidence (performance vs. business case)
- [ ] Updated roadmap and funding posture are captured
- [ ] A communication plan for affected teams and customers is included

## Entry criteria

- Commercial performance review complete
- Benefits realization report available
- Roadmap refresh in hand

## Exit signal

The next Discover cycle begins with a clear mandate — or the product sunsets gracefully.
