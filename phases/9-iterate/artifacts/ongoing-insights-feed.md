# ongoing insights feed

_Produced by: Continuous discovery_

**Business outcome supported:** close the loop — measure actual performance against the business case, decide continue/pivot/sunset, and feed validated learning back into Discover for the next cycle.

**Primary owner:** Business Analyst

**Stakeholders:** UI/UX Designer

**Accelerated MVP:** slim — a shared channel or doc is enough

## What this is

The continuous-discovery practice output — a running stream of research notes, user interview takeaways, behavioral observations, and hypotheses curated as they come in, not batched for quarterly deep-dives.

## Why it matters for the Business Analyst

Big-bang research is expensive and usually late. A continuous feed keeps the team grounded in emerging user reality all the time, and it shortens the time from "we hear this" to "we acted on this." It is also the primary input from Iterate back into the next Discover cycle.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Research sessions are logged with summaries
- [ ] User observations and quotes are captured
- [ ] Emerging hypotheses are recorded with their supporting evidence
- [ ] Surprises (expectation-vs-reality gaps) are called out
- [ ] Entries are tagged by theme and persona and list proposed follow-up experiments or interviews

## What it contains

- Research sessions and summaries
- User observations and quotes
- Emerging hypotheses and their evidence
- Surprises (expectation-vs-reality gaps)
- Follow-up experiments or interviews proposed
- Tags for theme and persona

## Inputs you rely on

- Scheduled interview cadence
- Support/VoC trend analysis
- Adoption metrics and usage analytics
- UX research plan and tools
- Customer Success observations

## Who consumes it

- Product Owner — seeds the next-cycle opportunity backlog
- UI/UX Designer — informs ongoing design work
- Sponsor — calibrates strategic narrative
- Next Discover cycle — enters as validated signal

## Common pitfalls

- Notes without synthesis; raw data never surfaces as insight
- Interview cadence halts during delivery crunch; feed goes quiet
- Insights tag to the researcher, not a searchable theme — nobody finds later
- No connection to backlog; nothing ever changes from the insight
