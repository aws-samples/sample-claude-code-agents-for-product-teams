# NPS & health scores

_Produced by: Customer success check-ins / NPS_

**Business outcome supported:** close the loop — measure actual performance against the business case, decide continue/pivot/sunset, and feed validated learning back into Discover for the next cycle.

**Primary owner:** Customer Support

**Stakeholders:** Sales & Marketing

## What this is

The latest NPS results and refreshed account health scores — promoters, detractors, verbatims, and the per-account composite score used to target retention work. Refreshed on a defined cadence.

## Why it matters for the Customer Support / Success team

NPS is noisy on a single response but powerful over a cohort. Combined with health scores, it tells you where to deploy save-plays, which customers are advocacy candidates, and whether product changes are moving sentiment. These are your steady-state retention levers.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] NPS score and distribution by segment are reported
- [ ] Promoter and detractor verbatims are included
- [ ] Change versus the prior period is shown
- [ ] Per-account health score and tier are assigned
- [ ] Save-play and advocacy candidate lists are produced

## What it contains

- NPS score and distribution by segment
- Promoter and detractor verbatims
- Change vs. prior period
- Per-account health score and tier
- Save-play and advocacy candidate lists
- Cohort cuts (by plan, tenure, segment)

## Inputs you rely on

- NPS survey results
- Usage telemetry and adoption metrics
- Support ticket volume and sentiment
- Renewal and expansion status
- Executive engagement logs

## Who consumes it

- CSM and Support leadership — intervention targeting
- Product Owner — sentiment-weighted feedback for roadmap
- Sales & Marketing — advocacy and referral pipeline
- Sponsor — customer-health signal in business reviews

## Common pitfalls

- Sample bias when only engaged users respond
- NPS headline without verbatim analysis; reason goes missing
- No closed-loop follow-up with detractors
- Health score ignored when it disagrees with CSM gut feel
