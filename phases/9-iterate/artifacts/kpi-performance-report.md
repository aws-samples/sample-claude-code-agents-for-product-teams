# KPI performance report

_Produced by: KPI / success-metric tracking_

**Business outcome supported:** close the loop — measure actual performance against the business case, decide continue/pivot/sunset, and feed validated learning back into Discover for the next cycle.

**Primary owner:** Product Owner

**Stakeholders:** Business Analyst

**Accelerated MVP:** required — this is the primary MVP learning

## What this is

The PO's and BA's joint rollup of product KPIs — adoption, retention, activation, revenue-per-user, task completion — against targets set in Define. Narrated, not just graphed.

## Why it matters for the Product Owner

KPIs are the shared language between Product, Sponsor, and the rest of the company. A crisp performance report gives you evidence to back roadmap decisions, forces honest conversation about what is and isn't working, and anchors the next-cycle opportunity backlog.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] KPI actuals are reported against the targets set in Define
- [ ] Segment and cohort breakdowns are included
- [ ] Trend and inflection points are identified
- [ ] Drivers, confounders, and qualitative context (releases, market, campaigns) are narrated
- [ ] Recommended actions for the roadmap are stated

## What it contains

- KPI actuals vs. targets
- Segment and cohort breakdowns
- Trend and inflection points
- Drivers and confounders
- Qualitative context (releases, market, campaigns)
- Recommended actions for roadmap

## Inputs you rely on

- KPI definitions and approved business case
- Connected KPI data pipeline
- Adoption metrics and feedback insights
- Experiment results
- Business performance report from Operate

## Who consumes it

- Sponsor — portfolio decisions
- Product Owner — updates the roadmap
- Business Analyst — profitability and benefits analyses
- Design, Engineering, Sales — shared view of outcomes

## Common pitfalls

- Headline KPIs hide critical segment shortfalls
- Targets quietly adjusted instead of honestly reporting shortfall
- Report published without narrative; readers draw wrong conclusions
- No linked actions; numbers change, roadmap doesn't
