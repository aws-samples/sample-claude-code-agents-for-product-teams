# user feedback log

_Produced by: Document user feedback_

**Business outcome supported:** close the loop — measure actual performance against the business case, decide continue/pivot/sunset, and feed validated learning back into Discover for the next cycle.

**Primary owner:** Customer Support

**Stakeholders:** Sales & Marketing

**Accelerated MVP:** slim — a shared doc or channel collecting feedback is enough

## What this is

The captured log of qualitative feedback users volunteer outside support tickets — feature requests, praise, complaints, anecdotes from CSM calls, sales conversations, and community channels. Structured enough to be analyzed later.

## Why it matters for the Customer Support team

Support and Sales hear customers daily. If that signal lives only in agent memory, it never influences the product. A simple, disciplined log is how CS and Sales turn thousands of conversations into an input for Product that feeds feedback insights and the next-cycle backlog.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Each feedback item has context and a source noted
- [ ] Customer account and segment are recorded
- [ ] Sentiment and intensity are captured
- [ ] Entry is classified as ask, complaint, or observation and tagged by theme/feature area
- [ ] Follow-up status (resolved, escalated, monitored) is tracked

## What it contains

- Feedback item with context and source
- Customer account and segment
- Sentiment and intensity
- Ask vs. complaint vs. observation
- Tags for theme and feature area
- Follow-up status (resolved, escalated, monitored)

## Inputs you rely on

- Customer calls, emails, and chat
- Field sales and SE conversations
- Community forums and social channels
- QBR notes and advisory board feedback
- Support tickets that include broader feedback

## Who consumes it

- Business Analyst — feedback insights report
- Product Owner — next-cycle opportunity backlog
- Product Marketing — refreshed messaging
- Customer Success — relationship context

## Common pitfalls

- Logged without account or segment context — can't weight by value
- Single loud account dominates the log; quiet signal missed
- Tagging inconsistent between reps; analytics noisy
- Closed-loop missing; customer never hears back
