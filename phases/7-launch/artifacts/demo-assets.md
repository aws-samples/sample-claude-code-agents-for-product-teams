**Accelerated MVP:** optional when no external demos are planned

## What this is

Purpose-built demo environments and sample data that make the product show well — seeded tenants, realistic example datasets, pre-configured dashboards, and scripted happy-path flows that reliably exercise the pitch story.

## Why it matters for the Developer

Sales engineers and PMM will demo this product dozens of times a week; the demo environment either sells or it embarrasses. As the Developer, a small investment in stable, realistic, resettable demo assets pays back in reduced live-support fire-drills and cleaner bug reports from the field.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Demo tenant(s) are provisioned with representative data and permissions in place.
- [ ] Reset and re-seed scripts return the environment to a named, known state.
- [ ] Sample datasets cover each key persona and use case on the pitch story.
- [ ] Scripted demo flows align step-by-step with the pitch deck.
- [ ] Ownership and refresh cadence are named for the demo environment.

## What it contains

- Demo tenant(s) with representative data and permissions
- Reset/re-seed scripts to return to a known state
- Sample datasets covering key personas and use cases
- Scripted demo flows aligned with the pitch deck
- Access management for SEs and partners
- Ownership and refresh cadence

## Inputs you rely on

- Pitch deck and demo script from Sales & Marketing
- Feature documentation and user guides
- Data inventory & classification (no real customer data)
- API specification and test fixtures
- Approved design patterns for environment isolation

## Who consumes it

- Sales engineers — live demos in deals
- Product Marketing — recorded demos and explainer videos
- Training teams — hands-on certification exercises
- Partners — demo into their own pipelines

## Common pitfalls

- Demo data that leaks real customer fragments — compliance risk
- Fragile demo environment that breaks between refreshes
- Demos that show features the core product doesn't support at the chosen tier
- No ownership; demo rot sets in within weeks of launch
