# go/no-go decision package

_Produced by: Launch stage-gate coordination_

**Primary owner:** Project Manager

**Accelerated MVP:** optional when the go-no-go is a PO and tech-lead conversation

## What this is

The PM-assembled package that the go/no-go meeting reviews: status, readiness evidence, known issues, risk posture, rollback plan.

## Why it matters

A structured package makes the go/no-go decision about evidence, not vibes. It also makes the decision repeatable on future launches.

## What it contains

- Readiness summary and open items
- Risk posture and mitigations
- Support, ops, and comms readiness attestations

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Readiness summary enumerates open items with owners.
- [ ] Risk posture is documented alongside the mitigation for each risk.
- [ ] Support, ops, and comms readiness attestations are captured from each function.

## Entry criteria

- Verify phase complete
- Launch-readiness status pack ready

## Exit signal

The go/no-go meeting can make a crisp decision.
