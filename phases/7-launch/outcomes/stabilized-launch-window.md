# stabilized launch window

_Produced by: Launch war room / hypercare_

**Primary owner:** Release Manager

**Stakeholders:** Site Reliability Engineer

## What this is

The product is live and operating within expected bounds; the hypercare team stands down gradually as signals stabilize.

## Why it matters

Launch isn't over when deploy finishes. A launch window ends when the product behaves predictably and support load is in normal range.

## What it contains

- SLO performance during the window
- Incident summary
- Transition-to-steady-state criteria met

## Definition of Done

- [ ] SLO performance across the launch window is captured against target.
- [ ] Incident summary lists each incident with severity and resolution status.
- [ ] Transition-to-steady-state criteria are documented and confirmed met.

## Entry criteria

- Deploy executed
- Hypercare team engaged
- Launch-day monitoring in place

## Exit signal

Operate becomes the home phase; hypercare wraps.
