# go/no-go decision

_Produced by: Launch readiness review / go-no-go_

**Primary owner:** Product Owner

**Stakeholders:** Release Manager, Product Sponsor

**Accelerated MVP:** required — the explicit call to ship is needed

## What this is

The final decision — go, no-go, or conditional — on whether to execute the launch. Owned by Sponsor, PO, and Release Manager.

## Why it matters

This is the last cheap stop-point before release. Making it formal means the authority to stop is exercisable until the moment before deploy.

## What it contains

- Decision with rationale
- Conditions or caveats attached
- Communications triggered by each outcome

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Decision is recorded with its rationale.
- [ ] Conditions or caveats attached to the decision are written down.
- [ ] Communications triggered by each outcome are specified.

## Entry criteria

- Decision package complete
- Compliance go/no-go and PRR sign-off in hand

## Exit signal

Deploy executes, slips, or cancels — explicitly.
