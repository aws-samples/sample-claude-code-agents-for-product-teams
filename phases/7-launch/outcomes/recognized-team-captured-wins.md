# recognized team & captured wins

_Produced by: Team recognition & celebration_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Accelerated MVP:** optional when the squad celebrates in-squad

## What this is

Explicit, Sponsor-led recognition of the team's contribution and capture of what went well for future reuse.

## Why it matters

Under-recognized launches erode team trust and make future mobilizations harder. Capture converts a success into institutional capability.

## What it contains

- Recognition artifact (email, ceremony, award)
- Brief capture of what worked — for reuse

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Recognition artifact (email, ceremony, or award) is produced and delivered.
- [ ] Brief capture of what worked is written down for reuse on future launches.
- [ ] Contributing teams and individuals are explicitly named in the recognition.

## Entry criteria

- Launch executed

## Exit signal

The team moves into Operate and the next cycle with morale intact.
