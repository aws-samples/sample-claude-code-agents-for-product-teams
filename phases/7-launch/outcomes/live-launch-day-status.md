# live launch-day status

_Produced by: Real-time launch metrics monitoring_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

## What this is

Real-time metrics and health signals visible to the Sponsor and launch team during the go-live window.

## Why it matters

Without real-time visibility, launch is a leap of faith. With it, rollback decisions are informed and wins are immediately visible.

## What it contains

- Connected KPI dashboard (wired in Launch)
- Operational health view (SLOs, errors, latency)
- Customer adoption signal view

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Connected KPI dashboard is live and visible to the Sponsor and launch team.
- [ ] Operational health view shows SLOs, errors, and latency in real time.
- [ ] Customer adoption signal view is accessible during the go-live window.

## Entry criteria

- KPI dashboard wired
- Telemetry verified in Verify

## Exit signal

The team can make live decisions rather than relying on post-hoc analysis.
