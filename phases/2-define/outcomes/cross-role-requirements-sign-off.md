# cross-role requirements sign-off

_Produced by: Review requirements_

**Primary owner:** All roles

## What this is

Every role that will execute against these requirements — engineering, QA, SRE, security, support, sales — has reviewed and agreed they can deliver their part.

## Why it matters

Sign-off from people who will actually do the work surfaces feasibility issues when they are cheap to fix. Skipping it means discovering them mid-build.

## What it contains

- Named sign-off per role with date
- Any caveats or conditions attached to each sign-off
- Record of concerns raised and resolved during review

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Each executing role has a named sign-off with date
- [ ] Caveats or conditions attached to each sign-off are captured
- [ ] Concerns raised during review show a resolution or an accepted disposition
- [ ] No required role is missing a sign-off entry

## Entry criteria

- Approved requirements exist
- NFRs and regulatory scope are known
- Dependencies and estimates are on the table

## Exit signal

The Define stage-gate can proceed without lingering doubt that 'someone downstream' will push back.
