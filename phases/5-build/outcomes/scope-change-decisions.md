# scope-change decisions

_Produced by: Scope discipline / change control_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Stakeholders:** Product Owner

## What this is

A record of every scope change the Sponsor and PO have decided on during build: add, drop, reshape — with rationale and impact.

## Why it matters

Scope changes happen; the question is whether they are tracked or absorbed silently. A tracked trail means the business case can be reconciled at launch.

## What it contains

- Change request, impact analysis, and decision
- Updated scope boundary
- Comms to affected stakeholders

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Every change request links to its impact analysis and decision
- [ ] The updated scope boundary is explicit
- [ ] Comms to affected stakeholders are recorded per decision

## Entry criteria

- Change-control process active (set up in Plan)
- BA scope-change impact analysis complete per change

## Exit signal

The scope being built is explicitly different from the original, not accidentally different.
