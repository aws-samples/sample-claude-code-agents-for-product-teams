# accepted increments

_Produced by: Progress review & increment acceptance_

**Primary owner:** Product Owner

**Stakeholders:** Project Manager

**Accelerated MVP:** required — someone has to say "this is the MVP, we're done for now"

## What this is

Work delivered during an iteration has been inspected against acceptance criteria and formally accepted by the Product Owner and Project Manager.

## Why it matters

Acceptance is the quality control of iterative delivery. Without it, 'done' is whatever the team says it is and incomplete work accumulates silently.

## What it contains

- Items accepted with date and reviewer
- Items rejected and reasons
- Stakeholder demo record

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Accepted items list the date and reviewer
- [ ] Rejected items cite the reason and return path to backlog
- [ ] Stakeholder demo record is attached or linked

## Entry criteria

- Increment meets Definition of Done
- Acceptance criteria are testable and met

## Exit signal

Accepted increments count toward scope complete; rejected items return to the backlog.
