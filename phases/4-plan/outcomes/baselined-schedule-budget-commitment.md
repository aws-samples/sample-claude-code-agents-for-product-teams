# baselined schedule & budget commitment

_Produced by: Approve delivery baseline_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Stakeholders:** Project Manager

**Accelerated MVP:** slim — a committed first-iteration window is enough

## What this is

The Sponsor and PM have formally baselined the plan: schedule, milestones, and budget. Any deviation now goes through change control.

## Why it matters

A baseline is how the organization detects drift. Without one, there is no signal — just an endlessly sliding 'plan'.

## What it contains

- Baselined critical-path schedule
- Budget baseline with spend plan
- Change-control thresholds and process

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Critical-path schedule is baselined and recorded
- [ ] Budget baseline includes a spend plan
- [ ] Change-control thresholds and process are documented
- [ ] Sponsor and PM sign-off are captured against the baseline

## Entry criteria

- Draft schedule complete
- Estimation credible
- Resources confirmed

## Exit signal

Every weekly status report compares actual to baseline, not to a moving target.
