# launch-readiness status pack

_Produced by: Launch-readiness status reporting_

**Business outcome supported:** evidence-based readiness to launch — functional, non-functional, operational, and documentation — validated in production-like conditions.

**Primary owner:** Project Manager

**Stakeholders:** _(none listed)_

**Accelerated MVP:** optional when there is no governance audience

## What this is

The rolled-up readiness pack the PM publishes on cadence through Verify — campaign progress, sign-offs landed and pending, top issues and defects, and the call-out for each role's blocking items. Feeds the Sponsor's launch-readiness assessment.

## Why it matters for the Project Manager

Verify is parallel, fast-moving, and political. A clean, regular pack keeps all roles oriented to the same truth and turns the final launch decision into a boring confirmation rather than a negotiation under time pressure.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Readiness RAG is reported per dimension (functional, perf, sec, ops, docs, GTM, support)
- [ ] Sign-off tracker shows owners and dates for each gate
- [ ] Top defects and risks are listed with owners
- [ ] Decision checkpoint dates and expected deliverables at each are documented
- [ ] Per-role asks for the cycle and an overall recommendation with launch-date confidence are stated

## What it contains

- Readiness RAG per dimension (functional, perf, sec, ops, docs, GTM, support)
- Sign-off tracker with owners and dates
- Top defects and risks with owners
- Decision checkpoint dates and what's expected at each
- Asks for each role this cycle
- Overall recommendation and confidence in current launch date

## Inputs you rely on

- Readiness assessment, security test report, performance test report
- PRR sign-off and compliance go/no-go state
- UAT/beta sign-off status
- Triaged UAT defects and regression test results
- Verify execution plan

## Who consumes it

- Product Sponsor — uses it to prep executive and board briefings
- Role leads — see what's blocking whom
- Release Manager — tracks path to launch-window commitment
- Adjacent PMs — coordinate cross-initiative launch impact

## Common pitfalls

- Pack is all RAG and no reasoning — readers guess
- Pending sign-offs not surfaced with explicit decision owners
- Weekly updates that lag reality by a week — decisions made on stale data
- Over-formal format that nobody reads vs. bullet-discipline that does the job
