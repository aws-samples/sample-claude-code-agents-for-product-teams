# warmed market & analyst briefings

_Produced by: Analyst / press / reference pre-briefing_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Stakeholders:** Sales & Marketing

**Accelerated MVP:** optional when the MVP is private

## What this is

Analysts, press, and reference customers have been pre-briefed by the Sponsor and Sales & Marketing, under embargo, so that launch-day coverage is informed rather than cold.

## Why it matters

Launch coverage quality depends on preparation weeks earlier. An unbriefed market produces uncertain narratives that Sales has to correct afterward.

## What it contains

- List of briefed analysts / press / customers
- Feedback from briefings and narrative adjustments

## Definition of Done

- [ ] List of briefed analysts, press, and reference customers is recorded
- [ ] Feedback from briefings and resulting narrative adjustments are documented
- [ ] Sponsor and Sales & Marketing delivery of briefings under embargo is confirmed

## Entry criteria

- Launch narrative and positioning ready
- Legal-approved messaging

## Exit signal

Launch-day coverage starts on the right foot, not from scratch.
