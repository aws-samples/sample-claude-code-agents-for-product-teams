# approved known-issues & risk acceptance

_Produced by: Approve launch risk posture_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Stakeholders:** Product Owner, Security & Compliance

## What this is

The Sponsor, PO, and Security & Compliance have formally accepted the list of known issues the product will launch with, and the compensating mitigations.

## Why it matters

Zero known issues is impossible. Explicit acceptance means launch risk is understood and shared — not a surprise for operations or support.

## What it contains

- Known-issue list with severity and customer impact
- Mitigations in place (workarounds, comms, monitoring)
- Planned fix timelines for each issue

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Known-issue list records severity and customer impact for each item
- [ ] Mitigations in place (workarounds, comms, monitoring) are documented per issue
- [ ] Planned fix timeline is assigned to every issue
- [ ] Sponsor, Product Owner, and Security & Compliance acceptance is recorded

## Entry criteria

- Verify testing complete
- Defect triage outcomes available

## Exit signal

Launch can proceed with eyes open; Support is prepared for the named issues.
