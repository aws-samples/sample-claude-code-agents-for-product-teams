# committed launch date

_Produced by: Approve launch window / date_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Stakeholders:** Release Manager

**Accelerated MVP:** required — a date forces completion

## What this is

The Sponsor and Release Manager commit to a specific launch date, making the internal and external commitments that follow it possible.

## Why it matters

Many downstream activities (press, enablement, legal, customer comms) cannot start in earnest without a date. Committing forces readiness to crystallize.

## What it contains

- Date, with time zone and go-live window
- Conditions under which the date can slip
- Authority to slip (and who decides)

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Date is stated with time zone and go-live window
- [ ] Conditions under which the date can slip are specified
- [ ] Authority to slip names who decides
- [ ] Sponsor and Release Manager commitment is recorded

## Entry criteria

- Launch-readiness assessment positive
- Operational readiness review underway or complete

## Exit signal

All launch-day plans are coordinated against a single date.
