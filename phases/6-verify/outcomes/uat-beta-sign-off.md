# UAT/beta sign-off

_Produced by: Pre-launch validation — UAT / beta_

**Primary owner:** QA/Tester

**Stakeholders:** Product Owner

## What this is

QA and the Product Owner sign off that user acceptance testing and/or beta validation shows the product meets its acceptance criteria under realistic use.

## Why it matters

Internal tests can be perfect while the product still fails for real users. UAT/beta is the last guard against that failure mode.

## What it contains

- UAT / beta results vs. acceptance criteria
- Defects found, fixed, or accepted as known issues

## Definition of Done

- [ ] UAT / beta results are documented against acceptance criteria
- [ ] Defects found are classified as fixed, deferred, or accepted as known issues
- [ ] QA and Product Owner sign-offs are recorded

## Entry criteria

- UAT/beta cohort recruited and onboarded
- Pre-launch validation executed

## Exit signal

The Sponsor can approve the launch cohort and risk posture with real-user evidence behind it.
