# vendor partnership agreement

_Produced by: Vendor / partner executive alignment_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Accelerated MVP:** optional when there is no vendor in scope

## What this is

A signed or committed agreement with the vendor/partner chosen in the build-vs-buy decision — the commercial, legal, and operational relationship is real.

## Why it matters

Without a signed agreement, build-vs-buy is theoretical. The team can block on vendor onboarding, security review, or legal terms at Plan or Build.

## What it contains

- Commercial terms (price, SLA, exit, data)
- Security and compliance commitments from the vendor
- Integration and support touchpoints

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Commercial terms (price, SLA, exit, data) are captured.
- [ ] Vendor's security and compliance commitments are documented.
- [ ] Integration and support touchpoints are named on both sides.
- [ ] The agreement is signed or formally committed, not verbal.

## Entry criteria

- Build-vs-buy decision made
- Vendor risk assessment complete
- Legal review complete

## Exit signal

The team can engage the vendor as a working partner, not just a sales prospect.
