# confirmed scope/budget or change request

_Produced by: Budget & scope reality check_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Stakeholders:** Product Owner, Project Manager

**Accelerated MVP:** slim — PO-confirmed scope is enough

## What this is

After the reality of design surfaces cost and complexity, the Sponsor confirms the original scope and budget, or triggers a formal change request.

## Why it matters

Design is the last cheap place to adjust scope. Running into Plan or Build with a budget everyone knows is wrong is how initiatives fail quietly.

## What it contains

- Confirmation that scope and budget remain valid, OR
- Change-request package with new scope, new budget, and decision rationale
- Communication plan for affected stakeholders

## Definition of Done

- [ ] The document states clearly whether scope/budget are confirmed or changed.
- [ ] If changed, a new scope, new budget, and decision rationale are attached.
- [ ] A communication plan for affected stakeholders is included.
- [ ] Sponsor sign-off is recorded on the outcome.

## Entry criteria

- Architecture and design-era cost model available
- Estimation refresh completed
- Business case re-run with design-era numbers

## Exit signal

Plan begins with a realistic baseline — not an aspirational one.
