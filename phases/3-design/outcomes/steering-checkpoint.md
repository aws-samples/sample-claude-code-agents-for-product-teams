# steering checkpoint

_Produced by: Steering committee review_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

## What this is

A scheduled steering committee review where cross-functional executives check progress, surface cross-portfolio issues, and confirm continued support.

## Why it matters

Steering is where portfolio-level trade-offs get made. A product bet can be architecturally sound and still need to pause because another initiative needs the same people or platform.

## What it contains

- Portfolio view and status against plan
- Decisions made or escalated at the steering level
- Next-review schedule

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Portfolio view shows status against plan.
- [ ] Decisions made or escalated at the steering level are recorded.
- [ ] Next-review date is scheduled.
- [ ] Continuation (or redirection) of the initiative is stated explicitly.

## Entry criteria

- Design-phase status and risks summarized
- Cross-portfolio dependencies identified

## Exit signal

The initiative has explicit steering continuation (or redirection).
