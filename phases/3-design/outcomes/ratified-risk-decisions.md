# ratified risk decisions

_Produced by: Risk appetite ratification_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Stakeholders:** Security & Compliance

**Accelerated MVP:** required — accepting a risk is a decision to record

## What this is

The Sponsor, with Security & Compliance input, has ratified the risk posture for the initiative: which risks to accept, mitigate, transfer, or avoid.

## Why it matters

Every project carries risk. Leaving the decisions implicit means risk owners are unclear and mitigations are assumed rather than funded.

## What it contains

- Risk item, classification, and chosen response
- Mitigation owner and deadline where relevant
- Residual risk accepted at executive level

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Every risk has a classification and chosen response (accept / mitigate / transfer / avoid).
- [ ] Mitigations have named owners and deadlines where relevant.
- [ ] Residual risk is explicitly accepted at the executive level.
- [ ] Sponsor ratification is recorded on the outcome.

## Entry criteria

- Risk register exists
- Security & Compliance has surfaced their risk concerns

## Exit signal

Teams execute knowing which risks are watched, mitigated, or accepted.
