# validated business-value alignment

_Produced by: Design-to-business-case alignment review_

**Business outcome supported:** produce a build-ready design package — UX, architecture, data model, APIs, threat model, and integration map — with engineering signed off to build.

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Stakeholders:** Product Owner, Business Analyst

**Accelerated MVP:** optional when the squad confirms alignment in a working session

## What this is

A Design-phase review that confirms the emerging UX, architecture, scope, and cost are still consistent with the approved business case — or surfaces the delta before Build commitment. It is the checkpoint that prevents the build team from faithfully delivering a product whose economics no longer work.

## Why it matters for the Product Sponsor

You lead this review because Design is where decisions quietly inflate cost or shift scope in ways that erode NPV. Catching drift here is still cheap; catching it in Build means either writing off sunk cost or relaunching the business case from mid-air.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Current scope is compared explicitly to the approved MVP / charter.
- [ ] Updated ROI model is compared to the original with delta drivers called out.
- [ ] Target KPIs and success thresholds are confirmed or revised.
- [ ] Risks materialized or retired since Define are listed.
- [ ] A decision (continue / resize / re-baseline / kill) is recorded.

## What it contains

- Current scope vs. approved MVP / charter
- [Updated ROI model](updated-roi-model.md) vs. original [ROI model](../../1-discover/artifacts/roi-model.md)
- Confirmed or revised target KPIs and success thresholds
- Design-era cost and timeline deltas with drivers
- Risks that have materialized or retired since Define
- Decision: continue / resize / re-baseline / kill

## Inputs you rely on

- [Approved business case](../../2-define/outcomes/approved-business-case.md)
- [Updated ROI model](updated-roi-model.md) and [updated traceability matrix](updated-traceability-matrix.md)
- [Confirmed scope/budget or change request](../outcomes/confirmed-scope-budget-or-change-request.md)
- Architect's emerging cost view from [technical options assessment](technical-options-assessment.md)

## Who consumes it

- Product Sponsor peers and portfolio leadership — for reallocation decisions
- Product Owner and Business Analyst — adjust scope and benefits plan
- Project Manager — updates baseline commitments for Plan

## Common pitfalls

- Treating it as a pass-through check rather than a real decision
- Re-baselining silently without updating the business case or benefits plan
- No record of what changed and why — audit trail dies
- Running the review with stale financials from early Define
