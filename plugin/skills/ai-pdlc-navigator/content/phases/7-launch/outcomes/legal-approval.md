# legal approval

_Produced by: Legal & compliance final sign-off_

**Primary owner:** Security & Compliance

**Stakeholders:** Product Owner

## What this is

Legal and Security & Compliance's final written approval that the product meets legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations at launch.

## Why it matters

Legal approval at launch catches last-minute issues — new clauses, changed regulations, packaging decisions that shift exposure. Without it, the product ships with undocumented legal risk.

## What it contains

- Approval scope and limitations
- Any launch-blocking items resolved
- Open legal items with owners and deadlines

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Approval scope and limitations are stated explicitly.
- [ ] Every launch-blocking item is listed with its resolution status.
- [ ] Open legal items name owners and deadlines.

## Entry criteria

- Compliance readiness review complete
- Pricing & packaging decided

## Exit signal

The product can be sold and used without legal surprise.
