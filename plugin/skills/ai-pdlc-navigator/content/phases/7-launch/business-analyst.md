# Business Analyst in Launch

Launch is where your benefits-tracking discipline goes live. You stand up the pre-launch baseline, wire the live KPI dashboard, and make sure impacted business teams actually changed their processes. Without this, nobody can tell in a month whether the bet worked.

## What you do

- Capture the pre-launch metrics baseline with Product Owner so lift can be measured against a real starting point.
- Set up the live launch KPI dashboard so Sponsors, POs, and operators see the same numbers.
- Roll out process changes to impacted internal business teams so the new way of working actually sticks.
- Ensure evidence and traceability from requirements through to live KPIs holds up post-launch.

## Artifacts you own

- [pre-launch metrics baseline](artifacts/pre-launch-metrics-baseline.md) — the "before" snapshot that unlocks lift measurement.
- [rolled-out process changes](artifacts/rolled-out-process-changes.md) — the confirmed adoption of new processes by affected teams.

## Artifacts you contribute to

You don't contribute to others' artifacts this phase.

## Outcomes you drive

- [live launch KPI dashboard](outcomes/live-launch-kpi-dashboard.md) — the working dashboard wired to real traffic.

## Who you work with

- **Product Owner** — bi-directional on baseline sequencing and KPI definitions.
- **Site Reliability Engineer** — bi-directional on KPI data pipeline wiring.
- **Developer** — bi-directional on telemetry emission and dashboard connections.
- **Affected internal teams** — bi-directional on process rollout and change adoption.

## Handoff into [Operate](../8-operate/README.md)

You exit Launch with a working baseline and dashboard. Carry these into Operate as the backbone of the business-performance report, ongoing benefits tracking, and support/VoC trend analysis. Your job in Operate is to turn live telemetry into credible answers about whether the business case is being earned.
