# internal kickoff announcement

_Produced by: Initiative kickoff & internal announcement_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

## What this is

An internal announcement that the initiative is officially underway, signed by the Sponsor, that names the team, goals, and governance.

## Why it matters

Announcement makes the project real inside the organization — it converts rumor into stakeholder awareness and invites the help the team will need.

## What it contains

- What we're building and why
- Team, Sponsor, PO, and core roles
- How other teams can engage or raise dependencies

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Announcement states what is being built and why
- [ ] Team, Sponsor, PO, and core roles are named
- [ ] Engagement path for other teams to raise dependencies is specified
- [ ] Sponsor signature is captured on the announcement

## Entry criteria

- Resources approved
- Baseline committed
- Stakeholder comms plan in place

## Exit signal

Other teams know the initiative exists and where to go with questions.
