# compliance go/no-go

_Produced by: Compliance readiness review_

**Primary owner:** Security & Compliance

## What this is

Security & Compliance separately signs off (or withholds sign-off) on whether the product meets its compliance obligations and is ready to serve real customer data.

## Why it matters

Compliance is a regulatory, not engineering, decision. Rolling it into the general go/no-go loses the specific accountability needed if something is later found lacking.

## What it contains

- Compliance decision with referenced controls and evidence
- Any open compliance actions and deadlines

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Compliance decision is stated with referenced controls and evidence
- [ ] Any open compliance actions list owners and deadlines
- [ ] Security & Compliance sign-off (or withhold) is recorded

## Entry criteria

- Compliance evidence collected per plan
- Security testing and audit evidence complete

## Exit signal

Compliance has a standing record it can hand to auditors.
