# Sales & Marketing in Verify

You are here to warm the market and land the cohort. Verify is your pre-launch ramp — analysts, press, and reference customers get pre-briefed, UAT / beta users get recruited, and launch assets get finalized so Launch is execution, not scramble.

## What you do

- Pre-brief analysts, press, and reference customers jointly with the Sponsor so launch-day coverage is warm.
- Recruit and onboard the UAT / beta cohort jointly with Customer Support.
- Finalize launch collateral, battlecards, demo scripts, and internal enablement materials.
- Stay close to the Product Owner and launch-readiness status pack so positioning matches what's actually shipping.

## Artifacts you own

- [recruited UAT cohort](artifacts/recruited-uat-cohort.md) — co-owned with Customer Support.

## Artifacts you contribute to

- [warmed market & analyst briefings](outcomes/warmed-market-analyst-briefings.md) — Product Sponsor owns; you run the program.

## Outcomes you drive

You don't drive outcomes this phase — you input into others'.

## Who you work with

- **Customer Support** — co-owns UAT cohort recruitment and onboarding.
- **Product Sponsor** — co-owns analyst briefings; sets the narrative altitude.
- **Product Owner** — confirms shipping scope so positioning stays honest.
- **Technical Writer** — aligns on launch assets that reference user-facing documentation.

## Handoff into [Launch](../7-launch/README.md)

In Launch you own the announcement, marketing collateral, customer communications, training, and sales-process cutover. You know Verify is done when analysts are warmed, the UAT cohort is active, and launch assets are approved and staged for release.
