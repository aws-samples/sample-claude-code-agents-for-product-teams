# UAT coordination & results

_Produced by: UAT coordination with business users_

**Business outcome supported:** evidence-based readiness to launch — functional, non-functional, operational, and documentation — validated in production-like conditions.

**Primary owner:** Business Analyst

**Stakeholders:** _(none listed)_

## What this is

The BA's run of UAT with business users — planning, scenario coverage, session logs, findings, and the structured outcome that feeds UAT/beta sign-off. Evidence that real users, not just QA, validated the product against intent.

## Why it matters for the Business Analyst

Requirements are only as true as the users' ability to use them. Running UAT rigorously — with real workflows, real data, real people — is how you catch the "works as specified but doesn't actually help" gap and deliver a signal that speaks to benefits realization, not just test pass rates.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] UAT scope and participant roster are documented
- [ ] Scenario catalog is tied to benefits and KPIs
- [ ] Session logs, issues raised, and observations are recorded
- [ ] Decisions on in/out-of-scope findings and process-change items for operate teams are captured
- [ ] Sign-off status is recorded by participant segment

## What it contains

- UAT scope and participant roster
- Scenario catalog tied to benefits and KPIs
- Session logs, issues raised, and observations
- Decisions on in/out-of-scope findings
- Process-change items for operate teams
- Sign-off status by participant segment

## Inputs you rely on

- Testable acceptance criteria and traceability matrix
- Benefits plan and KPI definitions
- Validated staging environment and test data
- Support process model
- Recruited UAT cohort

## Who consumes it

- UAT/beta sign-off artifact consumes these results
- Product Owner — scope decisions on UAT-flagged items
- Customer Support and Sales & Marketing — understand real-user confusion points
- Tech Writer — refines docs based on confusion observed

## Common pitfalls

- Testers used as QA extensions — no real-world workflows exercised
- Findings logged without severity or scope disposition
- Sign-off collected verbally; no evidence trail
- Process-change items ignored until after launch
