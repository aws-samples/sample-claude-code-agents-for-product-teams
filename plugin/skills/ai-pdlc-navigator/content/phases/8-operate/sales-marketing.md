# Sales & Marketing in Operate

Operate is where the commercial engine earns or misses the business case. You drive revenue and ARR tracking with the Sponsor, run win/loss analysis so the field sharpens, and partner with Customer Support on renewal and expansion so NRR targets are hit, not hoped for.

## What you do

- Drive revenue and ARR tracking with the Product Sponsor so the commercial scorecard is trustworthy.
- Run win/loss analysis to figure out why deals win or slip — and route learnings back to Product.
- Co-drive renewal and expansion management with Customer Support so retention is proactive.
- Maintain field enablement — battlecards, pricing, competitive intel — through the operating cadence.

## Artifacts you own

- [win-loss insights](artifacts/win-loss-insights.md) — the structured win/loss readout that feeds both sales coaching and product.

## Artifacts you contribute to

- [revenue report](artifacts/revenue-report.md) — Product Sponsor owns; you own the CRM and pipeline side.
- [renewal & expansion bookings](artifacts/renewal-expansion-bookings.md) — Customer Support owns; you own the deal-cycle side of renewal/expansion.

## Outcomes you drive

You don't drive outcomes this phase — you input into others'.

## Who you work with

- **Product Sponsor** — bi-directional on revenue, pipeline, and commercial posture.
- **Customer Support** — bi-directional on renewal, expansion, and customer health.
- **Product Owner** — bi-directional on win/loss signal and roadmap implications.
- **Business Analyst** — bi-directional on margin, CAC/LTV, and benefits work.

## Handoff into [Iterate](../9-iterate/README.md)

You exit Operate with commercial evidence — revenue trajectory, win/loss patterns, retention posture. Carry that into Iterate's user feedback log, NPS work, and feedback insights — the field signal you carry is high-leverage input into the next cycle's opportunity backlog.
