# Customer Support in Operate

Operate is your phase. You run activation, health scoring, QBRs, renewals, expansion, ticket triage, and the known-issues surface that the whole company leans on. Your job is to protect and grow recurring revenue while keeping cost-to-serve honest — the retention engine of the product.

## What you do

- Drive customer onboarding and activation so users hit value milestones.
- Score customer health and maintain the at-risk list.
- Run QBRs and customer check-ins, building the renewal and expansion pipeline.
- Manage renewals and expansion with Sales & Marketing so retention is proactive.
- Triage support tickets, troubleshoot customer issues, and resolve or escalate.
- Maintain the known-issues list so Sales, Support, and Product speak from the same baseline.
- Tag and categorize support tickets so the dataset is analyzable by Business Analyst.

## Artifacts you contribute to

You don't contribute to others' artifacts this phase.

## Outcomes you drive

You don't drive outcomes this phase — you input into others'.

## Who you work with

- **Sales & Marketing** — bi-directional on renewal, expansion, and customer comms.
- **Business Analyst** — bi-directional on ticket tagging quality and VoC trend analysis.
- **Technical Writer** — bi-directional on doc gaps and knowledge-base curation.
- **Developer** — bi-directional on escalation of reproducible bugs.
- **Site Reliability Engineer** — bi-directional on incident comms to customers.

## Handoff into [Iterate](../9-iterate/README.md)

You exit Operate with the richest real-customer signal in the company. Carry that into Iterate's user feedback log, NPS work, and the next-cycle opportunity backlog — your customers' unfiltered experience is the single most valuable input to the next discover cycle.
