# security/compliance approval

_Produced by: Security & compliance review_

**Primary owner:** Security & Compliance

**Stakeholders:** Architect, Site Reliability Engineer

## What this is

Security & Compliance approves the design, threat model, and pattern choices as sufficient to meet the applicable regulations and the organization's security bar.

## Why it matters

Approval at Design — not Launch — is where costs are still recoverable. Finding out at Verify that the design can't be secured kills timelines.

## What it contains

- Approval with scope (what was reviewed)
- Conditions or required mitigations before launch
- Audit-evidence plan linkage

## Definition of Done

- [ ] The approval states explicitly what was reviewed.
- [ ] Conditions and required mitigations before launch are listed.
- [ ] Audit-evidence plan is linked.
- [ ] Security & Compliance sign-off is recorded on the outcome.

## Entry criteria

- Threat model complete
- Security patterns selected
- Regulatory scope and data classification confirmed

## Exit signal

Engineering can proceed knowing the design passes security review.
