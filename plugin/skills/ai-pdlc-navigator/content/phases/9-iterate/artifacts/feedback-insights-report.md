# feedback insights report

_Produced by: Aggregate & analyze feedback_

**Business outcome supported:** close the loop — measure actual performance against the business case, decide continue/pivot/sunset, and feed validated learning back into Discover for the next cycle.

**Primary owner:** Business Analyst

**Stakeholders:** Product Owner

## What this is

The synthesized analysis across all customer-feedback streams — support tickets, NPS, interviews, win/loss, sales field notes, community — distilled into themes, severity, and opportunity. The organization's unified Voice of Customer.

## Why it matters for the Business Analyst

Feedback lives in silos by default; by the time a CEO asks "what do customers want?" the team is scrambling to piece it together. A unified insights report gives Product and Sponsor one authoritative view and keeps the Iterate priorities customer-grounded.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Themes include frequency, severity, and segment weighting
- [ ] Representative verbatims accompany each theme
- [ ] Quantitative signal (CSAT, NPS) is presented alongside qualitative themes
- [ ] Recommendations are tied to specific roadmap themes
- [ ] Trend versus the prior period is reported

## What it contains

- Themes with frequency, severity, and segment weighting
- Representative verbatims per theme
- Quantitative signal (CSAT, NPS) alongside qualitative
- Gap from product to desired outcome
- Recommendations tied to roadmap themes
- Trend vs. prior period

## Inputs you rely on

- Tagged support-ticket dataset and support/VoC trend analysis
- NPS & health scores and user feedback log
- Win-loss insights
- User research and continuous discovery (ongoing insights feed)
- Adoption metrics

## Who consumes it

- Product Owner — feeds updated roadmap and next-cycle opportunity backlog
- UI/UX Designer — redesign priorities
- Sales & Marketing — messaging refresh
- Sponsor — portfolio narrative

## Common pitfalls

- Loudest-channel bias; enterprise voice drowns SMB or vice versa
- Theme counts without severity or revenue weighting
- Verbatims cherry-picked to support a predetermined narrative
- Report without roadmap linkage; insight stays theoretical
