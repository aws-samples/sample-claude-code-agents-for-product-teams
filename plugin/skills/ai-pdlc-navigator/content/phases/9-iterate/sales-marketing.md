# Sales & Marketing in Iterate

Iterate is where the field closes the loop with product. You co-own the user feedback log and NPS work with Customer Support, turning a cycle of commercial and customer signal into positioning, pricing, and opportunity input for the next cycle.

## What you do

- Document user feedback with Customer Support — the structured VoC record.
- Run customer-success check-ins and NPS with Customer Support so retention signal is quantified.
- Feed win/loss and competitive patterns into the next-cycle opportunity backlog.
- Refine positioning, pricing, and competitive stance based on a cycle's worth of field evidence.

## Artifacts you own

- [user feedback log](artifacts/user-feedback-log.md) — the structured VoC record, with Customer Support.
- [NPS & health scores](artifacts/nps-health-scores.md) — the NPS and health readout, with Customer Support.

## Artifacts you contribute to

You don't contribute to others' artifacts this phase.

## Outcomes you drive

You don't drive outcomes this phase — you input into others'.

## Who you work with

- **Customer Support** — bi-directional on VoC, NPS, and retention signal.
- **Product Owner** — bi-directional on field learnings that belong in the updated roadmap.
- **Business Analyst** — bi-directional on feedback insights and trend analysis.
- **Product Sponsor** — bi-directional on commercial posture and portfolio decisions.

## Handoff into [the next Discover cycle](../1-discover/README.md)

You exit Iterate with a cycle of commercial and customer evidence. Carry that into the next Discover cycle's market analysis, competitive landscape, and pricing benchmark work — field signal is where positioning and pricing hypotheses get pressure-tested before the next cycle invests in them.
