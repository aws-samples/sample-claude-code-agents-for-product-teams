# 13. Sales & Marketing (Product Marketing + Sales Enablement)
- **Description:** Owns positioning, messaging, pricing, launch strategy, competitive intel, and sales readiness (battlecards, demos, certification).
- **Business purpose:** Converts product capability into pipeline and revenue.
- **Typically reports to:** VP Marketing/CMO (PMM); VP Sales/CRO (Enablement).
- **Project goals:** Validated positioning & pricing · certified sales team at GA · pipeline/win-rate lift · field feedback loop.


## AI assistance for this role

**Assistive AI (tight collaboration):**

- Drafts and critiques positioning and messaging across the [launch announcement](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/launch-announcement.md), [marketing collateral](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/marketing-collateral.md), [launch announcement emails/notices](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/launch-announcement-emails-notices.md), and battlecards in the [trained sales & support teams](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/trained-sales-support-teams.md) program.
- Scenario modeling for the [pricing & packaging plan](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/pricing-packaging-plan.md) against the [pricing benchmark report](../phases/1-discover/artifacts/pricing-benchmark-report.md); surfaces risks and wrinkles for field handling.

**Autonomous AI (fully delegated):**

- Continuous competitive and market intelligence feeding the [draft GTM plan](../phases/2-define/artifacts/draft-gtm-plan.md), [competitive landscape report](../phases/1-discover/artifacts/competitive-landscape-report.md), and battlecard refresh cycle.
- Win/loss synthesis into [win-loss insights](../phases/8-operate/artifacts/win-loss-insights.md); at-risk and renewal signals into the [renewal & expansion bookings](../phases/8-operate/artifacts/renewal-expansion-bookings.md) pipeline.
- Analyst and press coverage tracking with sentiment and share-of-voice against [analyst/press coverage](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/analyst-press-coverage.md).

**What AI never does:**

- Pricing ratification — the final [pricing & packaging plan](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/pricing-packaging-plan.md) must be signed by a human with pricing authority. AI models scenarios; it doesn't commit the company to a price.
- Launch-visible announcements and external-audience copy — [launch announcement](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/launch-announcement.md), [launch announcement emails/notices](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/launch-announcement-emails-notices.md), [marketing collateral](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/marketing-collateral.md) — ship only with human sign-off.
- Analyst and press engagement — [warmed market & analyst briefings](../phases/6-verify/outcomes/warmed-market-analyst-briefings.md), [analyst/press coverage](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/analyst-press-coverage.md) — the human AR/PR lead owns the relationship and the talking points.
- Revenue and commercial commitments — closed deals in the [live sales processes](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/live-sales-processes.md), renewal negotiations, and the [revenue report](../phases/8-operate/artifacts/revenue-report.md) numbers are owned and signed by humans.
- Messaging or positioning that contradicts a Sponsor-approved narrative or a Legal/Compliance constraint.

**Why:** This role's outputs commit the company to customers and the market — pricing sets the value exchange, launch messaging sets expectations, analyst engagement shapes long-cycle narrative. The cost of a wrong commitment is high and often irreversible (see the [HITL framework](../adoption/hitl-framework.md) on reversibility and customer-facing blast radius). AI sharpens options and scales preparation; a human with commercial authority signs what goes to the market.

## AI acceleration

These are examples of how AI can accelerate your work — not an exhaustive list. Calibrate them to your org's tooling and risk posture.

**Assistive AI (tight collaboration)** — you hold the pen, AI accelerates your judgment. Examples:

- **In your marketing-content platform**, shape positioning and messaging: draft and critique the [launch announcement](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/launch-announcement.md), [marketing collateral](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/marketing-collateral.md), and segment-specific [launch announcement emails/notices](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/launch-announcement-emails-notices.md).
- **In your sales-enablement platform**, construct battlecards and demo scripts in the [trained sales & support teams](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/trained-sales-support-teams.md) program; shape the [draft GTM plan](../phases/2-define/artifacts/draft-gtm-plan.md) against competitive context.
- **In your pricing / CPQ tool**, reason about pricing architecture: scenario modeling for the [pricing & packaging plan](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/pricing-packaging-plan.md) grounded in the [pricing benchmark report](../phases/1-discover/artifacts/pricing-benchmark-report.md); surface risks and wrinkles for field handling.
- **In your analyst / press relations tool**, prepare analyst and reference-customer briefings for [warmed market & analyst briefings](../phases/6-verify/outcomes/warmed-market-analyst-briefings.md); anticipate questions and shape narrative.
- **In your CRM**, synthesize win/loss patterns into a structured [win-loss insights](../phases/8-operate/artifacts/win-loss-insights.md) report — surface signals for product and packaging adjustments.

**Autonomous AI (fully delegated)** — AI runs without you in the loop; you set policy and review on cadence. Examples:

- **Scanning public sources and social/analyst feeds**, maintain current competitive and market intelligence; route material changes into the [draft GTM plan](../phases/2-define/artifacts/draft-gtm-plan.md) and battlecard updates.
- **In your marketing-content platform**, personalize [launch announcement emails/notices](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/launch-announcement-emails-notices.md) and segment communications at scale using approved templates and guardrails.
- **In your CRM**, transcribe and tag sales calls; synthesize loss-reason taxonomy feeding [win-loss insights](../phases/8-operate/artifacts/win-loss-insights.md); surface at-risk deals and renewal signals into the [renewal & expansion bookings](../phases/8-operate/artifacts/renewal-expansion-bookings.md) pipeline.
- **In your analyst / press relations tool**, track [analyst/press coverage](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/analyst-press-coverage.md) in near real time; sentiment analysis and share-of-voice reporting on the launch.
- **In your sales-enablement platform**, keep enablement content consistent across battlecards, one-pagers, and demo scripts; flag drift when product changes.
