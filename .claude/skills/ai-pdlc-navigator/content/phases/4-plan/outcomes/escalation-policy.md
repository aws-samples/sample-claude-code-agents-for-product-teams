# escalation policy

_Produced by: Set risk appetite & escalation thresholds_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

## What this is

An explicit policy: which risks and issues reach the Sponsor, how fast, through whom. Sets the risk appetite for the initiative.

## Why it matters

Without policy, PMs escalate everything (noise) or nothing (surprise). Policy makes the Sponsor's attention a predictable, scarce resource.

## What it contains

- Severity levels and response SLAs
- Escalation path by role
- Risk appetite statement (what's acceptable without escalating)

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Severity levels are defined with response SLAs
- [ ] Escalation path is specified by role
- [ ] Risk appetite statement names what's acceptable without escalating
- [ ] Sponsor approval is recorded against the policy

## Entry criteria

- Risk register exists
- Governance cadence set up

## Exit signal

The team knows exactly when to involve the Sponsor, and the Sponsor knows what demands their attention.
