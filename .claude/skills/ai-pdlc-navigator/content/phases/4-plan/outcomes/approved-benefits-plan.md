# approved benefits plan

_Produced by: Approve benefits realization plan_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

## What this is

The Sponsor approves the BA's benefits realization plan — what benefits, when, and how they'll be measured — as the commitment against which the initiative will be judged.

## Why it matters

Approved benefits convert the business case from a slide into a tracked commitment. Without approval, benefits realization reviews in Iterate become debates about what we 'really' promised.

## What it contains

- Benefits list with metrics and target values
- Measurement cadence and owner
- Dependencies on upstream activities (telemetry, dashboards)

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Benefits list records metrics and target values for each benefit
- [ ] Measurement cadence and owner are named per benefit
- [ ] Dependencies on upstream activities (telemetry, dashboards) are captured
- [ ] Sponsor approval is recorded against the plan

## Entry criteria

- KPI definitions in place
- Benefits plan drafted by BA

## Exit signal

Every benefit is owned, measured, and tied to a review cadence.
