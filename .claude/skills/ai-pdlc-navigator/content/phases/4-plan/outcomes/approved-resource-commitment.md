# approved resource commitment

_Produced by: Approve staffing & resourcing plan_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

## What this is

The Sponsor has formally committed the people, environments, and money the delivery plan requires — not just in theory, but on the floor of the organization.

## Why it matters

Phantom teams are the single most common cause of slipped plans. Approval forces the commitment to be real.

## What it contains

- Staffed roles with names and start dates
- Environment and infra commitments
- Any conditional resources and their triggers

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Every staffed role lists a named person and start date
- [ ] Environment and infrastructure commitments are recorded
- [ ] Conditional resources are paired with an explicit trigger
- [ ] Sponsor approval is captured against the commitment

## Entry criteria

- Staffing & resourcing plan drafted
- Technical skills requirement input from Architect

## Exit signal

Team onboarding can begin against a real roster.
