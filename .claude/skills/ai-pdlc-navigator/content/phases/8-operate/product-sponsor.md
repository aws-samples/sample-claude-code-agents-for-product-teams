# Product Sponsor in Operate

Operate is where the business case actually gets earned. You chair the operational and commercial reviews, own the top-line revenue and unit-economics view, and make the Sev-1 external-comms calls when the service fails. Your job is to turn Launch's promise into a retained, profitable customer base — portfolio-level accountability.

## What you do

- Review operational KPIs and SLO performance so reliability stays tied to commercial expectation.
- Approve Sev-1 external comms — the voice the company speaks with when the product breaks publicly.
- Track revenue and ARR with Sales & Marketing so the commercial engine stays on record.
- Review unit economics and margin with Business Analyst and Architect so the product is profitable, not just popular.
- Chair the business review meeting with Product Owner to convert operational signal into portfolio decisions.

## Artifacts you own

- [operational performance review](artifacts/operational-performance-review.md) — the recurring KPI/SLO readout that anchors the operating cadence.
- [revenue report](artifacts/revenue-report.md) — the commercial scorecard, produced with Sales & Marketing.
- [margin & CAC/LTV report](artifacts/margin-cac-ltv-report.md) — unit economics, produced with Business Analyst and Architect.

## Artifacts you contribute to

You don't contribute to others' artifacts this phase.

## Outcomes you drive

- [approved incident comms](outcomes/approved-incident-comms.md) — your approval on Sev-1 external communications.
- [business review decisions](outcomes/business-review-decisions.md) — your decisions, made with Product Owner.

## Who you work with

- **Product Owner** — bi-directional on business review decisions and roadmap posture.
- **Sales & Marketing** — bi-directional on revenue, pipeline, and retention.
- **Business Analyst** — bi-directional on margin, CAC/LTV, and benefits tracking.
- **Architect** — bi-directional on cost-to-serve and unit economics.
- **Site Reliability Engineer** — bi-directional on SLO performance and incident posture.

## Handoff into [Iterate](../9-iterate/README.md)

You exit Operate with a hard-evidence read on performance vs. business case. Carry that into the Iterate portfolio investment decision — iterate, pivot, double-down, or sunset when warranted — and bring the reforecast discipline so the next cycle's ask is grounded, not aspirational.
