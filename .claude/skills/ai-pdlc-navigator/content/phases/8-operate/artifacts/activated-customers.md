# activated customers

_Produced by: Customer onboarding / activation_

**Business outcome supported:** run a stable, SLO-meeting service while growing a profitable, retained customer base — the commercial engine of the product.

**Primary owner:** Customer Support

**Stakeholders:** _(none listed)_

## What this is

The operational record of customers successfully onboarded to the product — who hit activation milestones, when, and where they stalled. Tracked per cohort so onboarding improvements can be measured.

## Why it matters for the Customer Support / Success team

Activation is the leading indicator of retention. Customers who don't reach first-value in the promised window churn at a multiple of activated ones. Your onboarding program is the first and cheapest retention lever; measuring it turns support intuition into a repeatable process.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Activation milestones are defined per persona or plan with meaningful first-value criteria.
- [ ] Time-to-value is reported per cohort, not as a single aggregate.
- [ ] Onboarding touchpoints delivered are logged against each account.
- [ ] Stuck accounts are listed with the reason they stalled.
- [ ] Handoffs to Customer Success and feedback loops to Product are documented.

## What it contains

- Activation milestone definitions per persona or plan
- Time-to-value by cohort
- Onboarding touchpoints delivered
- Stuck accounts and why
- Handoffs to Customer Success for long-term care
- Loops back to Product for friction fixes

## Inputs you rely on

- User personas and journey maps
- Telemetry plan and activation events
- User guides and knowledge base
- Live support processes and support playbooks
- Live sales processes (handoff from Sales)

## Who consumes it

- Customer Success — identifies accounts needing intervention
- Business Analyst — activation input to ongoing benefits tracking
- Product Owner — prioritizes onboarding friction in Iterate
- Revenue and Finance — forecast expansion candidates

## Common pitfalls

- Activation defined by login rather than meaningful first-value
- Cohort tracking missing — can't tell if onboarding is improving
- Stuck accounts logged but not followed up
- No feedback loop to Product; the same friction recurs
