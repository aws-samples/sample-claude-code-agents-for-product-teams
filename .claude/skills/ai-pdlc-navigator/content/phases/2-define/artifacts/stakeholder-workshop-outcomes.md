# stakeholder workshop outcomes

_Produced by: Define-phase stakeholder workshops_

**Business outcome supported:** turn the validated opportunity into approved requirements, scope, NFRs, KPIs, regulatory scope, and a commercial model, with sized estimates ready to plan.

**Primary owner:** Project Manager

**Stakeholders:** _(none listed)_

## What this is

The consolidated record from Define-phase stakeholder workshops — decisions made, disagreements parked, action items owned, and open questions captured. It is the evidence that requirements and charter were co-produced, not imposed.

## Why it matters for the Project Manager

You produce this because without a record, the same decision gets re-litigated every week. A clean outcomes doc turns "I thought we agreed…" arguments into "we decided on [date], reference point is here."

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Workshop date, attendees, and absentees are recorded
- [ ] Decisions are listed with owner and effective date
- [ ] Unresolved disagreements are captured with parking-lot dispositions
- [ ] Action items name an owner and due date
- [ ] Artifact delta list specifies which charter, requirements, or plan changes follow

## What it contains

- Workshop date, attendees, and absentees
- Decisions made with owner and effective date
- Unresolved disagreements with parking-lot dispositions
- Action items with owner and due date
- Open questions needing investigation
- Artifact deltas (what changes in the charter, requirements, or plan as a result)

## Inputs you rely on

- Workshop agendas and pre-reads
- [Stakeholder map](../../1-discover/artifacts/stakeholder-map.md) for attendance strategy
- In-flight artifacts being reviewed (requirements, charter draft, roadmap)
- Prior workshop outcomes to avoid re-litigation

## Who consumes it

- Business Analyst and Architect — apply artifact deltas to their docs
- Product Owner and Sponsor — confirm decisions align with intent
- Project Manager (future you) — drives action items to closure and feeds the [cross-role requirements sign-off](../outcomes/cross-role-requirements-sign-off.md)

## Common pitfalls

- Summary without decisions — minutes that don't bind anyone
- Missing action owners or due dates — action items die in the doc
- No delta list, so artifact updates lag and trace becomes broken
- "Attendees absent" not tracked, so absent veto-rights surface later
