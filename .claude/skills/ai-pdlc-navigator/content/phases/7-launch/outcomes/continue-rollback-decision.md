# continue/rollback decision

_Produced by: Launch rollback decision authority_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Stakeholders:** Release Manager

## What this is

A live decision, owned by the Sponsor and Release Manager, made during or immediately after deployment: continue the rollout or roll back.

## Why it matters

Bad launches that continue out of momentum damage customers and brand. Someone must be explicitly empowered to pull the ripcord.

## What it contains

- Decision, timestamp, and trigger signals
- Communication issued to teams and customers
- Follow-up actions for rollforward or recovery

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Decision is recorded with timestamp and the trigger signals that drove it.
- [ ] Communication issued to teams and customers is captured or linked.
- [ ] Follow-up actions for rollforward or recovery are assigned to named owners.

## Entry criteria

- Deploy executed
- Real-time launch metrics visible
- Rollback runbook tested

## Exit signal

The product either moves into hypercare or rollback, with no ambiguity in between.
