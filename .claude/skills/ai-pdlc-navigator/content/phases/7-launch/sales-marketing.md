# Sales & Marketing in Launch

Launch is your phase to convert product capability into pipeline and revenue. You ship the battlecards, run the launch event, set pricing & packaging with the Product Owner and Sponsor, certify the field, and cut over the sales processes that make the commercial engine real.

## What you do

- Create marketing resources and battlecards so the field can sell from day one.
- Run the launch event — the external announcement with positioning, proof, and demos.
- Set pricing & packaging with Product Owner and Product Sponsor so commercial terms match product value.
- Deliver internal enablement and training with Customer Support so both teams are certified at GA.
- Execute customer communications — announcement emails and in-product notices.
- Cut over sales processes to the new motion so revenue can actually be booked.
- Co-engage analysts and press with the Product Sponsor.

## Artifacts you own

- [marketing collateral](artifacts/marketing-collateral.md) — battlecards, decks, one-pagers, demo scripts.
- [launch announcement](artifacts/launch-announcement.md) — the external launch event and narrative.
- [pricing & packaging plan](artifacts/pricing-packaging-plan.md) — the commercial terms you land with PO and Sponsor.
- [trained sales & support teams](artifacts/trained-sales-support-teams.md) — certified field and support, delivered with Customer Support.
- [launch announcement emails/notices](artifacts/launch-announcement-emails-notices.md) — direct customer communications at launch.
- [live sales processes](artifacts/live-sales-processes.md) — the cutover that makes the new motion real.

## Artifacts you contribute to

- [analyst/press coverage](artifacts/analyst-press-coverage.md) — Product Sponsor owns; you drive the PMM and AR motion.

## Outcomes you drive

You don't drive outcomes this phase — you input into others'.

## Who you work with

- **Product Sponsor** — bi-directional on pricing posture and analyst/press narrative.
- **Product Owner** — bi-directional on pricing, packaging, and launch sequencing.
- **Customer Support** — bi-directional on field enablement and cutover readiness.
- **Technical Writer** — bi-directional on messaging, collateral, and doc consistency.
- **UI/UX Designer** — bi-directional on in-product copy and visual assets.

## Handoff into [Operate](../8-operate/README.md)

You exit Launch with a certified field, live sales motion, and launched narrative in-market. Carry that into Operate's revenue/ARR tracking, win-loss analysis, and renewal/expansion motion — Launch was the opening; Operate is where pipeline, win rate, and NRR get earned quarter after quarter.
