# committed design partners

_Produced by: Design-partner / early-customer engagement_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Stakeholders:** Sales & Marketing

## What this is

Early customers, partners, or internal teams have formally committed to be the first real users of the product.

## Why it matters

Committed partners provide the pre-launch feedback loop and the initial launch pipeline. Waiting until Launch to recruit them is how products go live to empty rooms.

## What it contains

- Partner name and commitment level
- Scope of their participation (beta, reference, co-development)
- What they expect in return

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Each partner is named with a commitment level
- [ ] Scope of participation is stated as beta, reference, or co-development
- [ ] Expectations in return are recorded for each partner

## Entry criteria

- Sponsor/Sales&Marketing engagement underway
- Product maturity sufficient to demo or test

## Exit signal

Recruitment for UAT/beta in Verify is a named pipeline, not a cold start.
