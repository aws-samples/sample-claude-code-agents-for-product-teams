# unblocked dependencies

_Produced by: Unblock cross-org dependencies_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Stakeholders:** Project Manager

## What this is

A record of cross-organizational dependencies the Sponsor and PM have cleared — the other teams, vendors, or platforms the project needed to lean on.

## Why it matters

Dependencies that the team can't fix themselves are where the Sponsor's air cover earns its keep. Unblocking is a named deliverable, not a passive state.

## What it contains

- Dependency, blocker, and who unblocked it
- Any follow-up commitments from the other team

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Each entry names the dependency, the blocker, and who unblocked it
- [ ] Follow-up commitments from the other team are recorded
- [ ] Resolution is traceable to a date and decision owner

## Entry criteria

- Dependency list current
- Escalation policy in force

## Exit signal

Engineering velocity is not limited by external waits for more than the policy's escalation SLA.
