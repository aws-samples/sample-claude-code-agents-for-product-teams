# approved launch cohort

_Produced by: Approve beta / early-access cohort_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

**Stakeholders:** Product Owner

## What this is

The Sponsor and PO approve the specific customers, partners, or user groups who will be the first to receive the product.

## Why it matters

Launching to everyone is rarely right. Cohort selection manages launch risk, learning rate, and narrative — especially for net-new products.

## What it contains

- Named cohort with rationale
- Criteria for expansion to next wave
- Opt-out / rollback plan for the cohort

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Named cohort is listed with rationale
- [ ] Criteria for expansion to the next wave are defined
- [ ] Opt-out / rollback plan for the cohort is documented
- [ ] Sponsor and Product Owner approval is recorded

## Entry criteria

- UAT/beta results indicate readiness
- Customer Support and Sales are enabled for this cohort

## Exit signal

Launch execution targets a known audience with a known communication plan.
