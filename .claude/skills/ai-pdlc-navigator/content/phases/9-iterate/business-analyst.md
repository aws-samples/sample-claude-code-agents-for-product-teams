# Business Analyst in Iterate

Iterate is where you close the benefits-realization loop. You analyze adoption, aggregate feedback, run continuous discovery, root-cause any benefits shortfall, and reforecast the business case. Your output is the evidence that keeps the iterate / pivot / double-down / sunset decision honest.

## What you do

- Analyze feature adoption with Product Owner to see what's being used, by whom, and where.
- Aggregate and analyze customer feedback with Product Owner so insight sits on data, not anecdote.
- Run continuous discovery with UI/UX Designer so the insights feed is always on.
- Root-cause any benefits shortfall with Architect — why the bet underperformed.
- Reforecast the business case so the next cycle's funding ask is grounded.
- Contribute to profitability assessment and benefits realization with the Sponsor.
- Hand off insights to Discover with Product Owner.

## Artifacts you own

- [adoption metrics](artifacts/adoption-metrics.md) — the feature-adoption readout, with Product Owner.
- [feedback insights report](artifacts/feedback-insights-report.md) — the aggregated feedback analysis, with Product Owner.
- [ongoing insights feed](artifacts/ongoing-insights-feed.md) — continuous discovery output, with UI/UX Designer.
- [benefits-shortfall report](artifacts/benefits-shortfall-report.md) — the root-cause analysis, with Architect.
- [updated business case](artifacts/updated-business-case.md) — the reforecast evidence pack.

## Artifacts you contribute to

- [profitability assessment vs. business case](artifacts/profitability-assessment-vs-business-case.md) — Product Sponsor owns; you contribute the analysis.
- [benefits-realization report](artifacts/benefits-realization-report.md) — Product Sponsor owns; you contribute the benefits view.
- [KPI performance report](artifacts/kpi-performance-report.md) — Product Owner owns; you contribute KPI analysis.
- [next-cycle opportunity backlog](artifacts/next-cycle-opportunity-backlog.md) — Product Owner owns; you contribute insights curation.

## Outcomes you drive

You don't drive outcomes this phase — you input into others'.

## Who you work with

- **Product Owner** — bi-directional on adoption, feedback, and next-cycle hand-off.
- **Product Sponsor** — bi-directional on profitability, benefits, and reforecast.
- **Architect** — bi-directional on benefits shortfall and cost-driver root cause.
- **UI/UX Designer** — bi-directional on continuous discovery and insights feed.

## Handoff into [the next Discover cycle](../1-discover/README.md)

You exit Iterate with a reforecast business case, benefits evidence, and a curated insights feed. Carry that into the next Discover cycle's market analysis, validated problem statement, and opportunity sizing — one full cycle of real evidence beats any amount of re-analyzed greenfield hypothesis.
