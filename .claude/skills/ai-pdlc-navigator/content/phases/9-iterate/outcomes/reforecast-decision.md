# reforecast decision

_Produced by: Approve reforecast decision_

**Primary owner:** Product Sponsor

## What this is

When actual performance diverges from the original business case, the Sponsor decides whether to accept the BA's reforecast, adjust scope, or kill.

## Why it matters

Continuing to measure against an outdated business case corrupts every decision. Reforecast — with explicit approval — re-anchors the initiative to reality.

## What it contains

- Decision and new baseline if reforecast is accepted
- Any scope or investment changes triggered

## Definition of Done

- [ ] A decision (accept reforecast, adjust scope, or kill) is recorded
- [ ] The new baseline is defined if the reforecast is accepted
- [ ] Any scope or investment changes triggered by the decision are documented

## Entry criteria

- Benefits shortfall report in hand
- Updated business case drafted

## Exit signal

The team operates against a truthful plan — original or updated.
