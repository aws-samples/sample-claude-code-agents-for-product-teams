# Product Sponsor in Iterate

Iterate is where you make the call that matters most at portfolio level: iterate, pivot, double-down — or sunset when warranted. You hold the commercial performance view, ratify the reforecast, and align the organization around what's next. Your job is to turn evidence into decisive portfolio action.

## What you do

- Make the iterate / pivot / double-down (or sunset) call based on actual performance vs. business case.
- Chair the commercial performance review with Business Analyst and Architect so the decision is evidence-grounded.
- Drive the benefits realization review with Business Analyst.
- Approve the business-case reforecast so funding posture matches reality.
- Ratify the refreshed roadmap and any deprecation decisions with the Product Owner.
- Co-sponsor the post-launch retrospective with the Project Manager.

## Artifacts you own

- [profitability assessment vs. business case](artifacts/profitability-assessment-vs-business-case.md) — the commercial truth-telling, produced with Business Analyst and Architect.
- [benefits-realization report](artifacts/benefits-realization-report.md) — the benefits view, produced with Business Analyst.

## Artifacts you contribute to

- [updated roadmap](artifacts/updated-roadmap.md) — Product Owner owns; you ratify portfolio fit.
- [deprecation plan](artifacts/deprecation-plan.md) — Product Owner owns; you ratify the sunset decision when warranted.
- [retrospective report](artifacts/retrospective-report.md) — Project Manager owns; you sponsor and sign.

## Outcomes you drive

- [portfolio investment decision](outcomes/portfolio-investment-decision.md) — your call on iterate / pivot / double-down / sunset.
- [reforecast decision](outcomes/reforecast-decision.md) — your approval of the updated business case.

## Who you work with

- **Product Owner** — bi-directional on roadmap, deprecation, and next-cycle mandate.
- **Business Analyst** — bi-directional on profitability, benefits, and reforecast.
- **Architect** — bi-directional on commercial performance and modernization trade-offs.
- **Project Manager** — bi-directional on retrospective and project closure.

## Handoff into [the next Discover cycle](../1-discover/README.md)

You exit Iterate with a portfolio decision on record and a reforecast baseline. Carry that into the next Discover cycle as the executive mandate — "keep investing, expand here, or graceful sunset" — so opportunity framing and funding posture align from day one. Evidence-based portfolio discipline is how compound bets actually compound.
