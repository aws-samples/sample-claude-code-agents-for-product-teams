# 1. Product Sponsor (VP Product / CPO / GM / Executive Sponsor)
- **Description:** Sets strategic direction, owns the P&L, secures funding and air-cover, and arbitrates cross-functional trade-offs across a portfolio of initiatives.
- **Business purpose:** Ensures the initiative ladders up to corporate strategy and delivers portfolio-level business outcomes.
- **Typically reports to:** CEO (CPO/GM) or CPO/COO (VP Product).
- **Project goals:** Executive/board alignment on the bet · funding & staffing secured · portfolio KPIs hit · roadmap coherence preserved.


## AI assistance for this role

**Assistive AI (tight collaboration):**

- Sensitivity and scenario analysis on the [ROI model](../phases/1-discover/artifacts/roi-model.md), [margin & CAC/LTV report](../phases/8-operate/artifacts/margin-cac-ltv-report.md), and [revenue report](../phases/8-operate/artifacts/revenue-report.md) to frame decisions.
- Drafts and stress-tests narrative for the [business case](../phases/1-discover/artifacts/business-case.md), [strategic alignment statement](../phases/1-discover/artifacts/strategic-alignment-statement.md), and [board/investor launch narrative](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/board-investor-launch-narrative.md).

**Autonomous AI (fully delegated):**

- Continuous KPI and portfolio-health monitoring against the [operational performance review](../phases/8-operate/artifacts/operational-performance-review.md) with threshold-based escalation.
- First-draft [internal kickoff announcement](../phases/4-plan/outcomes/internal-kickoff-announcement.md), [internal launch announcement](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/internal-launch-announcement.md), and roll-ups of [governance briefings](../phases/5-build/artifacts/governance-briefing.md) for your review.
- Surfaces material external signals (analyst, regulator, competitor) for the [Discover exit decision](../phases/1-discover/outcomes/discover-exit-decision.md) and next-cycle framing.

**What AI never does:**

- Funding and investment commitments — [approved budget & investment commitment](../phases/1-discover/outcomes/approved-budget-investment-commitment.md), [approved resource commitment](../phases/4-plan/outcomes/approved-resource-commitment.md), [baselined schedule & budget commitment](../phases/4-plan/outcomes/baselined-schedule-budget-commitment.md), [reforecast decision](../phases/9-iterate/outcomes/reforecast-decision.md).
- Kill / pivot / double-down calls — [portfolio investment decision](../phases/9-iterate/outcomes/portfolio-investment-decision.md), [portfolio priority decision](../phases/4-plan/outcomes/portfolio-priority-decision.md), [business-case reconfirmation or reset](../phases/5-build/outcomes/business-case-reconfirmation-or-reset.md).
- Risk acceptance — [ratified risk decisions](../phases/3-design/outcomes/ratified-risk-decisions.md), [approved known-issues & risk acceptance](../phases/6-verify/outcomes/approved-known-issues-risk-acceptance.md), [escalation policy](../phases/4-plan/outcomes/escalation-policy.md).
- Launch authority — [go/no-go decision](../phases/7-launch/outcomes/go-no-go-decision.md), [continue/rollback decision](../phases/7-launch/outcomes/continue-rollback-decision.md), [committed launch date](../phases/6-verify/outcomes/committed-launch-date.md), [approved launch cohort](../phases/6-verify/outcomes/approved-launch-cohort.md).
- Incident comms approval — [approved incident comms](../phases/8-operate/outcomes/approved-incident-comms.md).
- Commercial commitments — [commercial model decision](../phases/2-define/outcomes/commercial-model-decision.md), [build-vs-buy decision](../phases/3-design/outcomes/build-vs-buy-decision.md), [vendor partnership agreement](../phases/3-design/outcomes/vendor-partnership-agreement.md).
- Executive review sign-off — [steering checkpoint](../phases/3-design/outcomes/steering-checkpoint.md), [architecture executive approval](../phases/3-design/outcomes/architecture-executive-approval.md), [approved business case](../phases/2-define/outcomes/approved-business-case.md), [Define exit decision](../phases/2-define/outcomes/define-exit-decision.md).

**Why:** These decisions commit the organization — capital, reputation, customer trust, regulatory posture — in ways that require an accountable human signer. AI informs the decision with sharper analysis, better-framed options, and continuous monitoring; it doesn't make the decision. This is the premise of the [HITL framework](../adoption/hitl-framework.md) applied at its most cautious end.

## AI acceleration

These are examples of how AI can accelerate your work — not an exhaustive list. Calibrate them to your org's tooling and risk posture.

**Assistive AI (tight collaboration)** — you hold the pen, AI accelerates your judgment. Examples:

- **In your presentation / narrative tools**, draft and stress-test the [business case](../phases/1-discover/artifacts/business-case.md), [strategic alignment statement](../phases/1-discover/artifacts/strategic-alignment-statement.md), and [board/investor launch narrative](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/board-investor-launch-narrative.md); surface counter-arguments before you take them to the room.
- **Working against your executive dashboard / BI tool**, explore sensitivity on the [ROI model](../phases/1-discover/artifacts/roi-model.md) and [margin & CAC/LTV report](../phases/8-operate/artifacts/margin-cac-ltv-report.md) to frame [reforecast decisions](../phases/9-iterate/outcomes/reforecast-decision.md) and [portfolio investment decisions](../phases/9-iterate/outcomes/portfolio-investment-decision.md).
- **In your strategic planning / portfolio tool**, compare options for [portfolio priority decisions](../phases/4-plan/outcomes/portfolio-priority-decision.md) and kill/pivot/double-down calls — option framing and second-order impact, not the decision itself.
- **In your internal announcement channel**, iterate on tone and framing for high-stakes comms like [approved incident comms](../phases/8-operate/outcomes/approved-incident-comms.md) before they go live.

**Autonomous AI (fully delegated)** — AI runs without you in the loop; you set policy and review on cadence. Examples:

- **In your executive dashboard / BI tool**, continuous monitoring of KPIs, the [revenue report](../phases/8-operate/artifacts/revenue-report.md), [margin & CAC/LTV report](../phases/8-operate/artifacts/margin-cac-ltv-report.md), and [operational performance review](../phases/8-operate/artifacts/operational-performance-review.md) with anomaly escalation against your thresholds.
- **Pulling from your strategic planning / portfolio tool and source systems**, roll up [governance briefings](../phases/5-build/artifacts/governance-briefing.md) and cross-portfolio status for your review.
- **Against external signals** (analyst coverage, regulatory changes, competitor moves), track what's material to the bet and route it to you — feeds the [Discover exit decision](../phases/1-discover/outcomes/discover-exit-decision.md) and the next cycle's refreshed view.
- **In your internal announcement channel**, generate first-draft [internal kickoff announcement](../phases/4-plan/outcomes/internal-kickoff-announcement.md) and [internal launch announcement](../phases/7-launch/artifacts/internal-launch-announcement.md) ready for your voice.
